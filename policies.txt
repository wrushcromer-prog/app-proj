## PTO Policy

Full-time employees receive 30 days of paid time off annually.
PTO requests must be approved by a manager at least 2 weeks in advance.
Unused PTO may be carried over up to 5 days into the next calendar year.

## Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 sick days per year.
Sick leave does not roll over between years.
Employees should notify their manager as soon as possible when taking sick leave.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week.
Additional remote work arrangements require manager approval.
Employees must maintain a reliable internet connection while working remotely.

## Travel Policy

Business travel must be approved in advance.
Flights under 4 hours should be booked in economy class.
Employees should use preferred vendors whenever possible.

## Expense Reimbursement

Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days.
Receipts are required for expenses over $25.
Reimbursements are typically processed within two payroll cycles.

## Health Benefits

Employees are eligible for health insurance on the first day of the month following their start date.
The company offers medical, dental, and vision coverage.
Dependents may be added during open enrollment.

## IT Security Policy

Employees must use multi-factor authentication.
Passwords must not be shared.
Lost or stolen devices should be reported within 24 hours.

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews occur annually.
Managers provide written feedback and development goals.
Compensation adjustments are reviewed following performance evaluations.
